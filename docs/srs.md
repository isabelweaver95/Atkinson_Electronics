# Atkinson Electronics Website SRS 

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of the website is to represent the full scope of the company’s services, establish credibility with commercial clients, and provide a clear access point to the existing circuit board/product website.

---

## 2. Goals

* Represent all areas of the business (not just circuit boards)
* Build trust with commercial clients
* Clearly explain services and capabilities
* Provide access to the circuit board/product site
* Generate leads (calls, emails, quote requests)
* Be simple and easy to maintain

---

## 3. Site Structure

### Main Navigation

* Home
* Services
* Industries / Projects
* Products / Circuit Boards
* Support / Service
* About
* Contact


---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Homepage

The homepage shall:

* Clearly explain what the company does
* Highlight the 3 main business areas
* Include a strong call-to-action (CTA)
* Include a dedicated section linking to the circuit board website
* Include trust signals (experience, industries, capabilities)

---

### 4.2 Services Pages

The website shall include service pages such as:

* Building automation / control systems
* Commercial controls integration
* HVAC controls
* Electrical / panel work
* Troubleshooting & upgrades
* Training / support

---

### 4.3 Circuit Board Portal (Important)

The website shall include a clear access point to the existing site.

**Example labels:**

* Visit Product Catalog
* Circuit Board Division
* Shop HVAC Interface Boards

**Placement:**

* In the navigation
* On the homepage
* On a dedicated “Products” page

---

### 4.4 Projects / Industries

Instead of a blog, this becomes the primary “dynamic” content section.

The site shall include:

* Industries served (commercial buildings, HVAC, etc.)
* Example projects
* Types of systems installed

---

### 4.5 Contact

The site shall include:

* Contact form
* Phone number
* Email
* Optional “Request a Quote” form

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

### Design

* Clean and professional
* Industrial / technical feel
* Not flashy
* Easy to navigate

### Performance

* Fast loading
* Minimal animations

### Maintainability

* Easy to update services and projects
* No requirement for frequent content updates

---

## 6. Key Design Insight

👉 Emphasize **Projects and Experience instead of Updates**

**Benefits:**

* Builds credibility faster
* More relevant for commercial clients
* Requires less maintenance

---

## 8. Development Recommendation

### Suggested Stack

* HTML / CSS / JavaScript (for now)
* Option to upgrade to Next.js later

### Notes

* CMS not required initially
* Content will be stable
* Focus on clarity and performance

---

## 9. Summary

This website should:

* Represent the full company (not just products)
* Maintain a professional, industrial feel
* Provide a clear path to the circuit board website
* Focus on credibility through services and projects
* Avoid unnecessary complexity like blogs
